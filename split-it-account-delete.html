Split-It - Account Deletion

You can permanently delete your Split-It account directly from within the app. This will remove all your personal data from our servers.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Split-It app on your device
  2. Navigate to Settings (tap the settings icon)
  3. Scroll to the bottom of the Settings page
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm your decision when prompted
  6. Re-authenticate with Google to verify deletion
  7. Your account will be permanently deleted

⚠️ What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your user profile and personal information (name, email, profile picture)
  • Your Firebase Authentication account
  • Your profile picture from cloud storage
  • Your membership from all groups
  • Expenses you created (to maintain group integrity for other members)

This action cannot be undone!

Data Retention

After account deletion:

  • Your personal data is immediately removed from our systems
  • Your contributions to existing groups may be anonymized but retained to maintain group expense calculations for other members
  • Cached data on your device will be cleared when you uninstall the app
