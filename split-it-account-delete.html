üî¥ Method 2: Delete Entire Account

Complete Account Deletion

To permanently delete all your data and your Split-It account:

  1. Open the Split-It app on your device
  2. Navigate to Settings (tap the settings icon)
  3. Scroll to the bottom of the Settings page
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Read the warning carefully
  6. Confirm your decision when prompted
  7. Re-authenticate with Google to verify deletion
  8. Your account will be permanently deleted

  • Device identifiers

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Can I recover my account after deletion?

No. Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. You would need to create a new account.

Will other users see my name after I delete my account?

Your profile will be removed, but past expense contributions may show as "Former Member" to maintain group balance accuracy.

How long does deletion take?

Most data is deleted immediately. Some cached data may take up to 48 hours to be fully removed from all systems.

What if I just want to leave a group?

You don't need to delete your account. Just open the group and select "Leave Group" to remove your membership while keeping your account active.

Can I download my data before deleting?

Yes! Use the "Export PDF Report" feature in each group to download your expense history before deletion.
